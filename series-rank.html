My top 3 series of all-time

Succession (2018)

Succession is an intense drama about familial relations and power in the modern world.

Maid (2021)

The series that really deserves to be seen, based on a true story of a woman trying to make a better life for herself and her toddler.

Modern Family (2009-2020)

I like this pretty funny sitcom, that's absolutely worth watching.
